One on One Fighter
Overview: "One on One Fighter" is a single-player combat game made with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The player takes on the computer in a series of rounds and must make strategic decisions to attack, defend, or attempt a finishing move based on the current state of the fight and their fighter's stats. The goal is to defeat the opponent and become the last fighter standing. This game offers an immersive and thrilling experience for players who enjoy fighting games and want to test their combat strategies and skills.

Next Steps: Improving user interface and adding animations for an enhanced player experience.
